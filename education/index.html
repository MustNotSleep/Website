Looking to Old Master works for inspiration, this figure drawing course will focus both on method, technique & materials, and on an aesthetic philosophy of classical beauty dating back to Ancient Greece. The coursework will include the study of anatomy, master copies, and live model work, including a session with multiple models. The live model drawings will all be from long, sustained poses to allow for a higher level of finish and deeper observation. The class is designed for all levels, and the instruction will start with fundamental basics. Beginners are welcome and encouraged.
