Education & Training

Introduction to Figure Drawing, Summer 2019

Instructor: Peter Green

Continuing Education at Cleveland Institute of Art

Figure or life drawing is the traditional practice of drawing from a live nude model. In this course you’ll learn about the proportions of human anatomy, how to make sight measurements, and about different drawing techniques for rendering the human form realistically. Each class will include demos and close instruction, but will allow for lots of direct practice: from quick gesture warm ups to extended poses.
